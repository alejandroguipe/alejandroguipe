## Research

### The Token Economy

I recently published the working paper:

**The Token Economy: Compute Power, Automation, and the Future of Work in an Ecosystem of Human Labor, Capital, and Artificial Intelligence**

This research explores how compute power and tokens are becoming central economic variables, connecting artificial intelligence, automation, labor economics, capital accumulation and productivity.

Main topics:

* Compute as a new factor of production
* Token costs and the economics of AI inference
* Automation vs. human labor from a task-based perspective
* The agentic cost paradox
* Productivity, labor markets and income distribution
* The future division of tasks between human labor, capital and AI
